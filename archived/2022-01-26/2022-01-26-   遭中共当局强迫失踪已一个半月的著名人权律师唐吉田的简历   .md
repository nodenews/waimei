[遭中共当局强迫失踪已一个半月的著名人权律师唐吉田的简历](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/01/blog-post_66.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，本网获悉遭中共当局强迫失踪已一个半月的著名人权律师唐吉田的简历，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">唐吉田</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1968</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日出生，吉林省延边朝鲜族自治州（以下简称“延边州”）敦化市人，北京市常住居民，前延边第二师范学校（后改制为延边第三中学校）教师，原执业律师，人权律师，《零八宪章》签署者，社会活动家，人权捍卫者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年，曾就读于东北师范大学政治系；<span lang="EN-US">1992</span>年毕业后，曾在吉林省延边州第二师范学校（<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年始被政府改制为延边第三中学校）从事教师工作<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年；自<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年起，调入延边州检察院从事刑事公诉工作，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年间代理了上百起重大公诉案件；<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年，通过律师资格考试，<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年，毅然辞去公职，先后在上海、深圳、北京、安徽省等地从事执业律师工作，曾为土地被非法征用受害者、强制拆迁受害者、上访者、三聚氰胺污染受害者等进行法律维权；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾接受过多起法轮功案件的代理辩护（含河北省石家庄市法轮功信仰者黄伟案、郝秋艳夫妇被非法拘禁案、辽宁省沈阳市吴业凤被控告“利用邪教破坏法律实施”案等）；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因推动北京市律师协会直选工作，而被律协宣布为非法，并将其作为主要发起人要求其离开所在律所；同年，曾签署了《零八宪章》；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月底，六四前夕，曾因要纪念六四而被北京市警方秘密绑架，先关押在海淀区的体育中心<span lang="EN-US">4</span>天，后又转移到朝阳区某宾馆继续关押到<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，才被释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">正因其频繁为被非法征用土地受害者、艾滋病受害者、法轮功信仰者、人权捍卫者以及其他弱势群体提供法律援助和帮助维权，随受到当局鲜明的打击和政治迫害；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2010</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，其律师营业执照被北京市司法局以“扰乱庭审纪律”为由吊销，但其仍不惧风险，继续活跃在维权领域，从事社会公共活动及多个相关维权案例（如广西北海白虎头村土地维权系列案、天津王宇律师被迫害案、重庆打黑硏讨会、反酷刑北海硏讨会、对高智晟和范亚峰等人遭酷刑问题进行联署等）的推动与策划；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2011</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，曾因当局打击中国茉莉花活动，而被北京市警方二度秘密绑架，并被辗转拘押在北京郊区数个地方，遭长时间剥夺睡眠、饥饿、体罚、殴打等酷刑虐待和审讯，导致其患上肺结核，至今仍需治疗中；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，曾因与多位人权律师前往黑龙江省鸡西市帮助被非法拘禁在“学习班”的法轮功信徒，而被鸡西市警方非法扣押，并处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">5</span>天，期间，惨遭<span lang="EN-US">10</span>根肋骨被打骨折，牙齿被打断；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，再遭离奇车祸，身体多处被骨折，但当其报案时警方却不予立案，交警也拒不调查，谎称监控器已坏；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，曾因身体疾患欲往香港求医，而被深圳罗湖海关的边检部门以“可能危害国家安全”为由拒绝出境；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，因其女儿在日本留学期间患肺结核病重，生命垂危，其遂急欲飞往日本探望和给予照顾，但却在福州机场出关时被边检部门拦截，并在北京市公安局指令下以“恐有危害国家安全”禁止出境；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，又因其预备出席次日的欧盟驻北京代表团举办的国际人权日活动，遂被北京市警方以涉嫌“危害国家安全罪”秘押，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，其之前被押期间，其曾遭受酷刑，其家人亦遭胁迫而不能发声；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
