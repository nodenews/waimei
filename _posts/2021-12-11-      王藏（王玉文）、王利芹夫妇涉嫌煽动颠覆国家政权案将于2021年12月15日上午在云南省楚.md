<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，本网获悉：王藏（王玉文）、王利芹夫妇涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权案将于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日上午在云南省楚雄彝族自治州中级法院开庭审理。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">著名诗人王藏及夫人王利芹因所谓“煽动颠覆国家政权”，王藏于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日被拘捕，王利芹于<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日被拘捕，二人于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月中旬被移送云南省楚雄州检察院审查起诉。当局指控王藏煽动颠覆国家政权罪名案件事实基本上就是他<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年那次被抓捕出来后所公开的言论、接受的采访、书写的诗歌文章、以及行为艺术。至今尚未开庭审理。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年起，因其积极参与多项维权民主活动、广交民主正义之士，并创作、推出了大量诗歌作品以及行为艺术，曾被北京当局多次警告威胁并驱离工作室和居住地，给其家庭造成诸多困扰。<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，因其在网上发布声援香港“雨伞革命”的打伞图片，随被北京市宋庄警方带走，其家被抄，之后被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘于北京市第一看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，被北京市当局以同罪名正式逮捕，其罪由包括行为艺术和诗歌、文章，网上发布声援香港“占中”图片，声援郭飞雄，声援建三江被虐打人权律师，在网上祭奠林昭，关注藏人自焚事件，声援新疆维吾尔族学者伊力哈木•土赫提、法轮功问题、纪念“六四”及揭批文革等多项内容。<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，被取保候审释放；据悉，其在被审讯期间遭受酷刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日早上<span lang="EN-US">4.30</span>分左右，被云南省楚雄市公安机关突然以涉嫌煽动颠覆罪由带走，后遭刑事拘留逮捕，被正式批捕的日期是<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日。现在羁押在云南楚雄市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王利芹：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，云南省昆明市人，又名王丽，职业不详，著名自由派先锋诗人及作家王藏（本名王玉文）之妻。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自从与自由派先锋诗人、作家王藏结婚以来，虽低调生活，但仍因其夫是异见诗人屡遭北京、昆明等地当局牵连与恐吓；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因其夫王藏被云南省楚雄市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”拘捕，而遭当局指使的不明人士抢劫，且被以若拒绝与警方合作，家即被逼迁和断水断电，甚至“会弄死孩子”等做要挟，致其因恐惧伤及<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个幼子而得精神分裂症；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，其夫王藏被云南省楚雄市警方突然以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”带走刑拘，自此失联，因其夫多日毫无下落，其被迫无奈，遂在各路网站（尤其是<span lang="EN-US">twitter</span>）携子为丈夫被抓捕呼吁，以求正义人士广泛关注与声援，后得知其夫已于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日被云南省楚雄州检察院以同罪名正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
