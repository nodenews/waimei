[贵州大学杨绍政教授煽动颠覆国家政权案已起诉  其被羁押期间遭办案人员酷刑虐待](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2022/03/blog-post_72.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，本网获悉：贵州省贵阳市人民检察院已经将杨绍政教授涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权案件起诉到贵阳市中级法院。杨绍政教授不认为自己有任何违法犯罪行为，杨绍政教授认为如果任由掌握公权的人不受制约的枉法滥权，每一个中国人都有可能受到自己所受到的可怕遭遇。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">此前杨绍政教授的辩护律师曾代理控告办案人员刑讯逼供、虐待被监管人，贵阳市人民检察院答复称控告不属实。<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，辩护律师再次向贵州省人民检察院提起了相关办案人员涉嫌构成刑讯逼供罪、虐待被监管人罪的控告。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">杨绍政</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，曾因网发揭露中共党政人员每年耗费<span lang="EN-US">20</span>万亿元人民币等的相关信息，而被贵州大学叫停授课，且此前就曾因涉政言论而两次被贵州省公安厅约谈和被明确警告在中共<span lang="EN-US">19</span>大期间“闭嘴”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年中共“十·一”期间，曾又遭贵州省政法委控制而软禁多日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，因杨绍政频发政治敏感言论，虽遭警方警告也不停息，遂再次被贵州省贵阳市花溪区警方秘密带走。自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
